SINGAM-3

Singam 3 is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language action masala movie written and directed by Hari. A sequel to Singam II (2013) and the third movie in the Singam franchise, it stars Suriya, Anushka Shetty and Shruti Haasan in the lead roles. It is the fifth collaboration of Suriya and Hari.
