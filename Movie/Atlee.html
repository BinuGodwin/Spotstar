ATLEE

Arun Kumar (born 21 September 1986), known professionally as Atlee, is an Indian film director, film producer and screenwriter best known for his work in Tamil films. He initially worked as an assistant director under S. Shankar in the films Enthiran (2010) and Nanban (2012). He made his directorial debut with the film Raja Rani (2013). He is one of the highest-paid directors in India.
