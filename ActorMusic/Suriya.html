SURIYA

Saravanan Sivakumar (born 23 July 1975), known by his stage name Suriya, is an Indian actor and film producer. He primarily works in Tamil cinema where he is one of the highest paid actors.He has received numerous awards including two National Film Awards,six Filmfare Awards South, five Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and two South Indian International Movie Awards.
