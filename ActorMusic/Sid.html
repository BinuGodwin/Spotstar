SID SRIRAM

Sidharth Sriram (born 19 May 1990[1]) is an Indian Carnatic musician, music producer, playback singer. He is an R&B songwriter[2] and has been working in the Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi, Marathi and English music industry. He regularly collaborates with his sister Pallavi Sriram, a Bharatanatyam dancer, and music directors
